### Part 1
Sami is A good boy. He has a cute little white cat. Her name is Bella. Bella is always very hungry. Sami gives her sweet good food.

### Part 2
Bella eats all the food fast. But then, poor Bella feels very bad. She is sick. Her hot head hurts. Sami is very sad for his cute cat friend.

### Part 3
Bella cannot eat any food now. Bella cannot drink cold clean water. She is very sick today. Sami puts his sick cat in a soft warm bed to sleep.

### Part 4
The next day, Bella is still very sick inside. She feels too cold. Sami calls the good animal doctor. "Please help my sick cat again," Sami says sadly.

### Part 5
The doctor comes quickly to Sami's home. He looks at quiet Bella in the bed. "Your cat is really sick," the doctor says. "She needs strong good medicine now."

### Part 6
The doctor gives Sami some new medicine. "Give this medicine to Bella every day," he says. "The medicine will help her sick head feel very much better soon."

### Part 7
Sami carefully gives the medicine to poor Bella. He also makes some warm chicken soup. Warm soup is always very good healthy food for a sick cat to eat.

### Part 8
Bella eats the nice warm soup. She takes the good red medicine fast. Then, she goes to sleep deeply in her small bed. Sleep helps her feel well again.

### Part 9
In the beautiful early morning, Bella wakes up. She is not sick at all now. Her head does not hurt today. She feels very well, active and very happy.

### Part 10
Sami is very happy now, too. His cute cat Bella is completely well. They eat more good food and play together. Bella is a very happy cat again today.
